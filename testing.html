Zipsy

Brought to you by the Developers at Cirtual, Zipsy is a location-based photo sharing app where users can post photos of things happening around them.

The catch is that they must pick at least 3 friends to reveal their identity to, before posting. To all other users, their photos and comments will be anonymous!

...And to make things even more exciting, all content will disappear after 48 hours!
