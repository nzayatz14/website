Album Pop

Introducing a quick and easy way to make an Album or Mixtape cover!

Upload your own image, add the filter of your choice, and add custom text.
Also includes the ability to add the "Explicit Content" label.
Then, share with your friends!

Available on iOS 7 or later.

Zipsy

Brought to you by the Developers at Cirtual, Zipsy is a location-based photo sharing app where users can post photos of things happening around them. The catch is that they must pick at least 3 friends to reveal their identity to, before posting. To all other users, their photos and comments will be anonymous! ...And to make things even more exciting, all content will disappear after 48 hours!
